The Dude's White Russian

In 1998 the Cohen brothers released The Big Lebowski and it was something of an overnight sensation.

The protagonist "The Dude", interpreted by Jeff Bridges is very famous for drinking white russians, spotted with a total of 7 glasses of the creamy looking thing during the 2 hour film.

You'll need

  • Vodka (2 oz/60 ml)
  • Coffee liqueur (1 oz/30ml)
  • Heavy cream (1 oz/30ml)

Just build in glass over crushed ice, leave cream last (do not incorporate).
