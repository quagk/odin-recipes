Jake's Perfect Sandwich

These are the the ingredients and stuff you'll need, most are easy to get but you might need some esoteric knowledge to put your hands on the last and most important one.

  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Ribeye
  • A torch
  • Cream cheese
  • Pickles
  • Dill
  • Tears for salt
  • Full loaf of bread
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • A bird from the window
  • Common cucumber
  • Sweet yellow onion
  • Sliced roma tomatoes
  • Bacon
  • Fresh Lobster Soul

Assembly

As for how to put it together you'll need instruction from the realm of creation above, though if you lack enlightenment you can follow the process below

  1. Wash a carrot (important)
  2. Sharpen your knife 6 times
  3. Vacuum seal the steak with the rosemary an thyme and into a 135Â° sous vide until ready
  4. Slice open your bread and torch both sides
  5. Cream cheese your bread and add the pickles and dill
  6. Dice your boiled eggs and add them alongside your cooked bird from the window
  7. Now throw in your sliced cucumber, onion and tomatoes
  8. Add your tears, steak and bacon
  9. For the most important part throw in your lobster soul
  10. If your sandwich isn't glowing by now you did something wrong, throw it away and start over.

If you ended up with a glowing sandwich that means you're worthy and can indulge in such marvelous creation, no other sandwich comes close to what you've just birthed.
